Config key: `business`

- `hideCurrency`: ([s]: []) array of currencies to hide from available business currencies
- `documents`:
  - `hide`: (b: false) controls whether to hide the documents section of business onboarding/settings

```json
"business": {
  "hideCurrency": [],
  "documents": {
    "hide": false
  }
}
```
